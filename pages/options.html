HOW TO PLAY

  • Add 1 point for every correct answer.
  • Subtract 1 point for every wrong answer.
  • 3 right answers in a row activates bonus
  • Bonus: Once active, next question is worth +3 or -3 points.
  • Game ends after 20 questions.
  • Easy: 15 second timer
  • Medium: 10 second timer
  • Hard: 5 second timer
